Our vision is about your vision

Vision apps analyze, detect, modify and gather information from image streams, live streams, video or single images. They’re becoming increasingly popular and are used by more and more devices and services.

The problem with vision apps is that they're tricky to develop and build because developers need to have at least some understanding of image analysis.

Our vision is simple, even though the technology behind is not. VisionAppster exists to create the tools and services developers need to write better code and build better apps - and more efficiently.

VisionAppster

is the brain child of founders who together have over 100 years of experience developing computer vision, machine vision, algorithms, and software.
