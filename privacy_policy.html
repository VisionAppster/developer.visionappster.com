PURPOSE OF PROCESSING PERSONAL DATA

The purposes of processing the personal data are the basic operation of VisionAppster’s services, including user support operations, service maintenance and technical support, responding to inquiries, organizing competitions, collecting statistics regarding service usage, and conducting scientific and market research.

VisionAppster may also use your information for the purposes of creating, administering and troubleshooting accounts, for invoicing, user account management, statistics and development purposes. Data may be used to enable user-to-user communication (e.g. via Development Forum) and to inform users of new products and services, including promotional offers. Personal data may be used to inform of VisionAppster services, such as sending a newsletter or informing about email campaigns or other ways of maintaining the customer relationship.

VisionAppster may use your information to prevent fraud, enforce the relevant terms of service or licenses, and protect users.

As part of data processing the data may be used for profiling purposes and to perform analytics and research aimed at improving the accuracy, effectiveness, usability, or popularity of products and services that VisionAppster provides.

VisionAppster may collect date of birth or personal / business ID, billing or financial account information and tax ID or other similar information for the purposes of 1) providing the VisionAppster Services for Users; and 2) paying a competition participant(s) or winner(s) an awarded prize, subject to competition rules.

The legal justification for processing personal data is fulfilling the contract between the parties or the legitimate interest of the controller, which is based on the relationship between the parties. The legal justification may also be the data subject consent, if the applicable legislation so requires.

You are not required to provide the personal information that we have requested, but, not providing the requested personal information may in many cases means that we will not be able to provide you with our products or services or respond to your queries.

In certain transactions, such as in VisionAppster Store where VisionAppster only provides the Store and transfer of payments by VisionAppster or third-party payment processor as further described in Store Seller’s terms, VisionAppster is in the role of data processor. In such events the data controller such as the seller is responsible for ensuring that it has the necessary rights and has acquired the necessary consents to process personal data. The Seller maintains privacy policy records as required by the data protection legislation.

RIGHTS OF THE DATA SUBJECT

The data subject has the rights according to the personal data legislation applicable in Finland, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), to inspect his/her personal information, change or request to change his/her information and under some circumstances, the right to request erasure of personal information.

The data subject has the right to receive confirmation from VisionAppster as to whether or not personal data concerning the data subject are being processed. The data subject is entitled to receive a copy of the processed personal data and the personal data undergoing processing. Whether the data subject makes the request by electronic means and he or she has not requested it by any other means, the information shall be provided by electronic means. The data subject has also the right to obtain from VisionAppster the rectification or erasure of personal data concerning him or her and the data subject has the right to prohibit the processing of personal data for direct marketing purposes.

If processing of personal data is based on consent, the data subject has the right to withdraw his / her consent. The withdrawal of consent may, however, affect the usability and functionality of the service in question. The withdrawal of consent shall not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

In certain cases, the data subject has also the right to request from the controller restriction of processing of personal data or otherwise object to processing. Furthermore, the data subject may require the transmission of the personal data, which he or she has provided to the controller in a machine-readable format based on the GDPR. All requests mentioned here shall be provided to the above-mentioned contact person of VisionAppster. Whether the data subject finds the processing of his or her personal data unlawful, he or she has the right to lodge a complaint to the authorities responsible for personal data protection.

COOKIE POLICY

VisionAppster uses cookies and similar tracking technologies such as Google Analytics on its website to help provide, protect and improve the VisionAppster services, to assist with navigation, and to analyse your use of our products and services. A cookie is a small piece of text that is stored on your browser or device by website. It helps the website to remember information about your visit, like your preferred language and other settings. You may refuse to accept cookies or delete cookies already stored on your computer through the settings of your web browser. If you choose to decline cookies, some parts of the service may not work as intended.
